SYMPHONIC BAND
The Nathan Hale High School Symphonic Band has a long history of musical excellence driven by a commitment to studying and performing the finest wind literature at the highest level. In March of 2011, the group performed at the 10th Annual University of Wisconsin – Milwaukee Concert Band Festival where the received superior ratings. Members of the Symphonic band participate in summer marching band camp, marching band and pep band in addition to formal concerts and WSMA Solo and Ensemble.

WIND SYMPHONY
The Nathan Hale High School Wind Symphony is comprised of students who are interested in developing their musicianship at the high school level. The ensemble consists of mostly Freshmen, however, Sophomores, Juniors and Seniors also comprise this band and several students play a secondary instrument in this class. Members of the Wind Symphony participate in summer marching band camp, marching band and pep band in addition to formal concerts and WSMA Solo and Ensemble.

CONCERT BAND
The Nathan Hale High School Concert Band is comprised of students who are interested in developing their musicianship at the next level. Freshmen, Sophomores, Juniors and Seniors comprise this band and several students play a secondary instrument in this class. Members of the Concert band participate in summer marching band camp, marching band and pep band in addition to formal concerts and WSMA Solo and Ensemble.

JAZZ ENSEMBLE
The Nathan Hale Jazz Ensemble program is an enrichment activity and course, though it is mostly for saxophone, trumpet, trombone, piano, bass and percussion players. Membership in the Jazz Ensemble is open to all band students wishing to expand their musical backgrounds in this genre. This ensemble will meet every Tuesday and Friday morning from 6:50 a.m. - 7:30 a.m. weekly. The Jazz Ensemble will perform at formal concerts, as well as special activities as the year goes on. These dates will be made available to those performers as soon as they are finalized!
