MEET THE DIRECTOR
Ms. Kristi Wicihowski is in her seventh year as director of bands at her alma mater, West Allis Nathan Hale High School. She also serves the West Allis-West Milwaukee School District as band director at Lane Intermediate School.

At Hale, Ms. Wicihowski directs the Symphonic Band, Concert Band, Wind Symphony, Jazz Ensemble, “Marching Huskies,” and pep band. At Lincoln, she directs the band program at all grade and experience levels. Ms. Wicihowski is a 2002 graduate of the University of Wisconsin – Milwaukee (Bachelor of Fine Arts in Music Education and Trumpet Performance), and recently (Spring 2013) completed the Masters of Music Education – Instrumental Conducting program at UWM. She has studied trumpet with Wayne Cook and Kevin Hartman and conducting with Thomas Dvorak and John Climer. Ms. Wicihowski taught for six years in the Palmyra-Eagle Area School District, where she built the band program to include a fully comprehensive band program, with marching and concert performances on the state and national levels.

As a free-lance trumpet player, “Ms. W.” continues to be active in appearances all over southeastern Wisconsin, including performances with the Waukesha Symphony Orchestra, Violent Femmes and Gilberto Santa Rosa. Ms. W married her best friend in the summer of 2010 and revitalized her passion in Reggae music while honeymooning in Jamaica!! In her spare time, Ms. Wicihowski loves to watch major league baseball (Milwaukee Brewers, Boston Red Sox), college football (Georgia Bulldogs, Clemson Tigers, Iowa Hawkeyes) and of course the Green Bay Packers. Other interests include: running, cycling, golf, kickball, Ziggy Marley, Jimmy Buffett, traveling, and spending time with her family and Rat Terrier Puppy, Ziggy!
